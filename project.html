Our Projects

In the Eco-Maniacs club, you can assure yourself that you'll be having a ton of fun while doing work.  We conduct a variety of projects during every season.

Road and beach clean-ups are some of our more popular activities.  Beach clean-ups occur twice every year; once in the fall, and once in the spring.  The beach we choose to go to may vary from time to time.  We clean up the same road year after year.  All the dates and locations of the clean ups will be given out in meetings.

In addition to the clean-ups, we conduct educational programs at other environmental centers, set up craft booths at community events, and go on multiple different field trips.  These field trips can be whitewater rafting, snow tubing, ice skating, or even a treetops zip-line course!
